## Explanation of Analysis

Each SQL query in this project is designed to answer a real business question.

### Customer Retention
Measures how many users return within 30 days of their first purchase, indicating early engagement quality.

### Repeat vs One-Time Customers
Identifies which customer segment drives revenue and supports targeted retention strategies.

### Revenue Leakage
Quantifies revenue loss due to failed payments, highlighting operational inefficiencies.

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
Estimates the total value a customer brings over their active period.

### Churn Analysis
Detects inactive users to support re-engagement campaigns.
